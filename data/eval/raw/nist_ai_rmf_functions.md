# NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF 1.0) - Core Functions

The NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF 1.0) describes four core functions to help organizations address the risks of AI systems:

Govern: a culture of risk management is cultivated and present. Govern is a cross-cutting function that is infused throughout AI risk management and enables the other functions.

Map: the context is recognized and risks related to that context are identified.

Measure: identified risks are assessed, analyzed, or tracked using quantitative, qualitative, or mixed-method tools.

Manage: risks are prioritized and acted upon based on a projected impact.
