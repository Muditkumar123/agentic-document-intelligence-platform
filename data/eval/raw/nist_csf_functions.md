# NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) 2.0 - Core Functions

The NIST Cybersecurity Framework 2.0 organizes cybersecurity outcomes into six core Functions:

Govern: establish and monitor the organization's cybersecurity risk management strategy, expectations, and policy. Govern was added in CSF 2.0 and informs how an organization will achieve and prioritize the outcomes of the other five Functions.

Identify: help determine the current cybersecurity risk to the organization by understanding its assets, suppliers, and related risks.

Protect: use safeguards to prevent or reduce cybersecurity risk.

Detect: find and analyze possible cybersecurity attacks and compromises.

Respond: take action regarding a detected cybersecurity incident.

Recover: restore assets and operations that were impaired by a cybersecurity incident.
